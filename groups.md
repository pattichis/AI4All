# Form groups of 2 (up to 4) students.

# Basic rules for groups

## Actively Engage During Meetings
Minimize distractions during in-person and virtual meetings.​<br>

## Ensure Every Voice Is Heard and Valued.
Listen actively and respectfully.<br>​
Share the floor.​<br>
Avoid interrupting and allow space for quieter participants to share.​<br>

## Commit to Collaboration & Value Diverse Perspectives​
Share ideas openly and be willing to build on others’ contributions.<br>​
Embrace the range of experiences and expertise in the group.​<br>

## Be Constructive with Feedback​
Offer feedback that is specific, actionable, and kind.​<br>
Critique ideas, not people​. <br>

## Maintain Confidentiality​
Respect the privacy of discussions and unpublished ideas.​<br>
Only share outside the group with explicit permission.​<br>

## Focus on Solutions​
Approach challenges and disagreements with problem-solving mindsets.​<br>
Seek to resolve issues collaboratively and constructively.​<br>
Address misunderstandings with curiosity and care.​<br>

## Celebrate Progress​
Acknowledge individual and group achievements.​<br>
Take moments to appreciate the work and effort contributed by all.<br>

# Additional rules for teams
## Have fun with the assignments.
## Take turns discussing the code.
## Take turns with typing.
## Support each other.
## Respect each other.
## Allow others to talk.
